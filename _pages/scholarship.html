The Edinburgh Hoppers is proud to present a small scholarship scheme for our members. With the scholarship, we aim to provide support for a variety of needs such as:

  1. Professional and personal development: paying examination fees, enrollment fees for an online course, public speaking lessons, life skills coaching, etc.
  2. Living Costs: assistance for paying rent and utility bills, which can be tough in the pandemic.
  3. Travelling: this would involve requiring support for taking a Covid-19 test for essential travel (we will not provide support for any non-essential travel, as per travel regulations).

You do not necessarily need to fall into these categories to apply for a scholarship. However, you will be given the opportunity to elaborate on your situation in the application. The maximum amount we can potentially donate to one applicant is Â£200.

Who can apply?

All applicants have to identify as a gender minority in Informatics and be a part of the Hoppers mailing list.
